SUMMARY                                         *vim-railsdock*

Easily open the apidock.com page for method tips from Vim.

                                                *railsdock-:Api*
:Gem [{gem-name}]     Use :Api rails to open up rails on apidock.com.
                      If the cursor is located on a gem name you can
                      use :Api to open up the apidock page for that method.
